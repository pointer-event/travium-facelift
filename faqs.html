🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Travium.

1. What is Travium? ➕

Travium is an event management platform to help you create, manage, and promote your events easily.

2. How can I create an event? ➕

Just register, log in, and go to the "Create Event" page to set up your event details.

3. Is there a fee for using Travium? ➕

Creating events is free. We only charge a small fee for paid ticket transactions.

4. How do payments work? ➕

Travium supports secure online payments via credit card, e-wallets, and bank transfers.

5. Can I refund tickets? ➕

Refund policies depend on each organizer, but Travium provides tools to process refunds easily.

6. Is my data safe? ➕

Yes, we use encryption and follow best practices to keep your data secure at all times.
